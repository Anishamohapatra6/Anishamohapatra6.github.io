About

I am a Mechanical Engineering undergraduate at the National Institute of Technology Rourkela (Honours, CGPA 8.43/10), with experience spanning robotics research, optimisation for Urban Air Mobility (UAM), and industrial automation. My recent work includes developing optimisation frameworks for UAM and eVTOL operations and leading Industry 4.0 sensor integration projects in manufacturing.

My interests lie in robotics, control systems, and mechatronics, particularly in applying rigorous modelling and optimisation techniques to real-world systems. I aim to pursue graduate studies (M.S./Ph.D.) in robotics and controls, working at the intersection of algorithms and hardware.
